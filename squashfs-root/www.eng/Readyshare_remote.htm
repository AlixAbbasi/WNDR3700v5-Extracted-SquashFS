ReadyCLOUD
The ReadyCLOUD feature gives you remote access over the Internet to a USB storage device that is connected to your router's USB port.
How to Set Up ReadyCLOUD
Step 1: You need a ReadyCLOUD account. If you do not have one, click here to get one.
Step 2: On this page, enter your ReadyCLOUD user name and password to register your router and the USB devide that is connected to it with your account.
Step 3: Log back to into http://readycloud.netgear.com with your ReadyCLOUD account. The USB device that is connected to your router.
Note:You can use a supplementary remote application to make a secure connection from your computer or mobile device to the router's USB device. Log in to this remote application and you can access the USB device from anywhere. Click here to get the remote application.
Note: Without this client, you can brower your USB device contents but you cannot open files or make changes to them.
